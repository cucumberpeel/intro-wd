Methodology

Strong Athletes

Coach A specializes in balancing on-ice and off-ice training to help her students gain long-term strength. Her commitment to nurturing well-balanced skaters protects her students from injury and allows them to stay in the sport for years.

Strong Competitors

As a 4-time Regionals competitor culminating at the Novice level, Coach A has valuable physical and mental training to pass onto her students. She thoroughly prepares her competitive students in jumps, spins, and choreography, while ensuring that they are having fun competing.

Strong Foundations

Coach A passed X of Y of her US Figure Skating Tests with Honors, and she is determined to nurture equally strong skating skills in her students, regardless of their experience in the sport.

Strong Passion

Coach A believes that a firm passion for figure skating is the key to longevity. Even when her students encounter challenges, A's firm yet gentle coaching methods help children and adults alike maintain their love for the sport.
